input codes

Xbox inputs

Buttons binary ON/OFF
X = BTN_WEST
B = BTN_EAST
Y = BTN_NORTH
A = BTN_SOUTH

Left D-Pad -1 to 1
left/right = ABS_HAT0X
up/down = ABS_HAT0Y

Left Joystick 12bit?
left/right = ABS_Y
up/down = ABS_X

Right Joystick
left/right = ABS_RY
up/down = ABS_RX

Bumpers Binary ON/OFF
Left Bumper = BTN_TL
Right Bumper = BTN_TR

Triggers 8bit
left trigger = ABS_Z
right trigger = ABS_RZ

Joystick clicks ON/OFF
BTN_THUMBL
BTN_THUMBR

Middle Buttons
left = BTN_START
right = BTN_SELECT
